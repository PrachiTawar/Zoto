#Zoto – AI Food Discovery Chatbot 🍜🤖

Zoto is an AI-powered food discovery chatbot built for enhancing the user experience on food delivery platforms like Zomato.
It helps users discover dishes and restaurants based on their cravings, budget, location, preferences, and reviews through an intelligent conversational interface.

🚀 Features
🍕 Smart food recommendations
📍 Location-based restaurant suggestions
💸 Budget-friendly filtering
⭐ Review & rating-based recommendations
🧠 AI-powered conversational interaction
❤️ Personalized food discovery experience
💡 Problem Statement

Many users know what they feel like eating but struggle to decide where to order from. Traditional food delivery apps rely heavily on manual searching and scrolling.

Zoto solves this by acting as an AI food assistant that understands user cravings and instantly suggests relevant dishes and restaurants.
